#1 OBLIQUE STRATEGIES

July 7 23

An Oblique Strategy is an aphorism, koa, or short phrase meant to induce lateral thinking to aid creative processes. There have been a few versions produced over the years, usually in the form of a deck of cards, including atleast one web-based version before AVERY's.

INSTRUCTIONS

Seek guidance as a stuck creative. Press the button once. Read and apply your strategy. Do not draw another "card" until you are pursuing a new project.
